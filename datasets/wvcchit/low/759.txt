"The system shall have the ability to record the date and time (to the minute) of the consent, refusal, or deferral status as it relates to the administration of each immunization at the time of each encounter, including the following discrete data elements: decision, name of decider, status of decider (e.g. parent, self, legal guardian, medical power of attorney), and date/time(s). In the event of refusal of an immunization, the system shall allow recording of one or multiple discrete reasons for vaccine refusal."
